The AUDITIONS brings people with diffrent cultures and talents around Africa. The main aim of this show is to promote unity and develop local talents and make africa a better place .

The concerts brings together a community of people from different culture, race and ethnicity who comprise the entertainment industry in a different country every year.
Next year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advances the cause of Talent, free culture and knowledge.
So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open content and music - Producers, Music Directors, Music Promoters, D.J, gallaries, museums, archieves, governments and foundations, activists, and Content Creatives - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant free society together.
